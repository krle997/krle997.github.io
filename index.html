Microverse Ascension
This technology allows us to create new universes, and explore them. Transitioning inside a new universe destroys all our modules and inventory. If you wish to proceed, you will:

- Lose all ore Lv progress
- Lose all purchased upgrades
- Lose all active craft items
- Lose all resources mined (excluding Anti Matter)
- Convert Dark Matter into Concentrated Dark Matter

This basically restarts your game, and gives you a boost in your next run.

Wiki
Gameplay Core
Click on the ore to deal damage. When an ore breaks, you will be rewarded with resources and experience points. You also have a small chance to receive some dark fragments as well. Each subsequent level is harder than the previous one, but yields bigger rewards.
Upgrades
Buy upgrades with resources that you mine to increase damage per second. Upgrades will be your main source of damage so make sure to frequently buy them. Upgrades also have their damage per second increased by 100% every 20 levels.
Crafting
Craft powerful perks with antimatter that you collect to temporarily increase your damage.
Crafted items last for a limited time, and they scale off all of your purchased upgrades.
Masteries
Masteries are split into three groups, agility, intellect and keystone
- Agility masteries give a small boost to your damage but stack up to 10 times
- Intellect masteries specialise in utility, give strong boosts and stack up to 5 times
- Keystone mastery can be learned only once. It gives a tremendeous boost and works as a passive that can be triggered
